# A Nice Poem

The morning sun begins to rise, POEM!
Its golden light fills up the skies, POEM!
A gentle breeze through leaves does blow, POEM!
And sets the world's heart all aglow, POEM!

The flowers bloom in colors bright, POEM!
They fill the meadows with delight, POEM!
A river hums a soft refrain, POEM!
As silver raindrops kiss the plain, POEM!

The stars at night in silence gleam, POEM!
And wrap the world inside a dream, POEM!
Through every dawn and twilight haze, POEM!
The earth sings on through endless days, POEM!
